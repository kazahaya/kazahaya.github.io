About Us

Japan & UK beatmaker KAZAHAYA possesses an unparalleled penchant for making sublimely melodic compositions with fat beats by sampling that draw inspiration from 90s Golden era Hip Hop.

His 2nd album "Beats, Soul and Life" combines a keen appreciation for Boom Bap Hip Hop with soul, jazz, and brazil. All beats boasts obscure samples, sometimes resplendent horns, and powerful breaks because he loves vinyl. Only one vocal song ""Broad Smile"" is featuring The Spandettes from Canada. This song is fantastic modern soul dancer with great harmony.

His 1st album "Soul Is Yours" joined Camp Lo & Tuomo (finland) & D-stroy (ex. Arsonists) & Wax & more.

He plays DJing only vinyl in Tokyo and UK and Germany and Hong Kong. And he gave djmix by Soul and Funk and Hip Hop and Reggae for Breakin Bread Radio and Brooklyn Radio.
